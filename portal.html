About

Our connection is supplied by Sonic.net, which we highly recommend. The maximum speed they can offer at our location is 1Mbps upstream and 6Mbps downstream. The wireless network is served from a Linksys WRT54GL running Tomato, the WRT54GL is connected to a Soekris net4801 running pfSense. If you have further questions, you can email me at tyler@linux.com.

Rules

First, a couple of ground rules:

  • Do not hog the connectoin. This means no running BitTorrent or other file-sharing applications, and avoiding video sites like Hulu or Netflix. You can however watch videos on Prunetube as much as you want, after you click the button below

Violating the rules will likely get you banned from the network.
